	You may be wondering about this block, "why didn't he just use CV_CONVERT_TAG in ILLAGER_BEAST?"
	The answer is that CV_CONVERT_TAG can't seem to remove arguments properly;
	if I remove just the 5FINGERS body part itself, the eyelids, lips and teeth also get removed,
	and removing HUMANOID_JOINTS deletes the lungs. I have no idea why.

	And I can't just do this exact block in ILLAGER_BEAST because there is no GO_TO_TAG there,
	and as far as I know, the BODY token has to be positioned before tissues are added.
	Same issue applies for the skin/hair blocks just below.
